SNAKE

Welcome to the game of Snake!

Rules:

  • Don't run the snake into the wall, or his own tail: you die.
  • Use your cursor keys: up, left, right, and down.
  • Eat the colored apples to gain points.
  • You will need to click your "Refresh" button to start over.

Click the canvas and press in a direction (left, right, up, down) to begin.
